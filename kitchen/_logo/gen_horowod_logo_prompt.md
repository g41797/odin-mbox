A concept for a logo for the matryoshka project.

The logo should evoke the main ideas of the project:
- Matryoshka dolls as a metaphor for layered architecture.
- "The endless inter-threaded game" - constant movement and communication.
- "Horowod" - a Russian round dance, symbolizing cooperation and flow.

The image should be a 512x512 pixel art logo.

It should feature 5 Matryoshka dolls of different sizes and colors, arranged in a circular "horowod" (round dance). The dolls should appear to be in motion, perhaps slightly tilted.

Glowing trails or sparks should connect the dolls, representing the "messages" or "data" being passed in the "endless game" of inter-thread communication.

The style should be simple and clean, fitting for a software project logo. The dolls should be stylized and easily recognizable.

The overall feeling should be one of dynamic, harmonious, and continuous movement.
